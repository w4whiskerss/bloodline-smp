# Bloodline SMP

Welcome to the Bloodline system.

Each player has **one active Bloodline**. Your Bloodline gives you passives and combat abilities that scale up to **Level 5**.

## How it works
- You start with 1 Bloodline
- Bloodlines level up through PvP progression
- Higher levels improve damage, effects, and cooldown scaling
- You can only have **1 active Bloodline** at a time
- If you switch bloodlines, your old active one is replaced
- You do **not** keep free access to the old one
- To get a previous Bloodline back, you must obtain that Bloodline's **Trait Potion** again
- If you switch back, the same rule applies to the Bloodline you just left
- On death, your active Bloodline level resets
- Trait and upgrade items can change your progression

## Ability controls
### Normal controls
- `Primary` -> `Shift + Right Click`
- `Secondary` -> `Shift + Left Click`
- `Special` -> `Shift + F`

### If you use the client hotkey mod
- `Primary` -> `Shift + V`
- `Secondary` -> `Shift + B`
- `Special` -> `Shift + N`

# Aqua

## Passive
- Water Breathing while in water
- Strength while in water
- Dolphin's Grace while in water
- All water buffs are removed when leaving water

## Primary Ability - Water Dash
- Dash forward at high speed
- Stronger dash in water
- Shorter dash on land
- Damages and knocks back players hit
- Cooldown: `60s`

## Secondary Ability - Suffocation Curse
- Curses a target with drowning pressure even on land
- Applies damage over time
- Applies slowness
- Can only be removed if the target enters water
- Duration: `10s`
- Cooldown: `3m`

## Special Ability - Tidal Surge
- Releases a wave around the player
- Knocks back nearby enemies
- Grants temporary buffs to Aqua
- Creates a temporary water area
- Can optionally send the user to the Aqua realm if enabled
- Duration: `10s`
- Cooldown: `5m`

# Spartan

## Passive
- Permanent Fire Resistance
- Higher levels can also grant passive Strength

## Primary Ability - Fireball
- Summons a held fireball at the Spartan's head
- Aim and launch it
- Explosion power scales with level
- Cooldown: `120s`

## Secondary Ability - Flaming Hands
- Melee hits apply a very strong burn
- Burn lasts a long time
- Water and Fire Resistance do not remove it
- Only the cursed player's kill can break the effect
- Duration: `3m`
- Cooldown: `10m`

## Special Ability - Hell Dominion
- Enter a full fire-powered state
- Gain Strength and Speed
- Hits apply stronger burn and extend burning
- Creates a fire aura that damages nearby players
- Can send up to `2` nearby players into the Nether
- Can optionally send the Spartan to a fire realm if enabled
- Duration: `10s`
- Cooldown: `5m`

# Earthian

## Passive
- Resistance is always active
- Reduced knockback
- Reduced fall damage
- Regeneration after standing still

## Primary Ability - Ground Slam
- Launch yourself upward
- Then slam down hard with forced gravity
- The landing explodes the ground below you
- You do not take slam fall damage
- Damage scales with level and fall height
- Cooldown: `90s`

## Secondary Ability - Root Prison
- Traps nearby players in the area
- Applies heavy slowness
- Applies mining fatigue
- Pulls enemies inward to stop escape
- Duration: `5s`
- Cooldown: `3m`

## Special Ability - Worldbreaker
- Enter a massive destruction state
- Summons a large amount of TNT around you
- The TNT explodes around the area
- The Earthian is protected from that explosion damage
- Meant for huge AoE pressure and chaos
- Cooldown: `5m`

# Voider

## Passive
- Gains a random daily potion buff
- The daily effect lasts the full cycle

## Primary Ability - Void Blink
- Instantly blink forward a short distance
- Can pass through players
- Your next hit shortly after deals bonus damage
- Also applies slight knockback
- Cooldown: `45s`

## Secondary Ability - Void Send
- Sends a target to a random safe location in the End
- The target lands on safe ground, not the void
- Limited charges with timed recharge
- Cooldown system is charge-based

## Special Ability - Void Flight
- Temporarily equips Elytra
- Lets the Voider fly and glide freely
- Restores the original chestplate after the ability ends
- Applies safety effects when needed after flight
- Duration: `5m`
- Cooldown: `30m`
